#############################################################
 SketchyCircles - Sketchable Fourier Transform - Android App
#############################################################

Express your drawing in terms of rotating complex
exponentials while on your Android device! 

To run this first make sure you have Android Studio 
installed on your computer and that it is connected to an
Android device. Then download this folder and 
all of its contents and open it in Android Studio.
Now build the app by pressing Ctrl+9 and then run it
by pressing shift+F10, with your Android device selected.

Enjoy!
